Brand Identity

Brand identity is the visible elements of a brand, such as color, design, and logo, that identify and distinguish the brand in consumers' minds.

Branding/Logo

A logo is generally a combination of typographies, graphics/symbols and colors. It is a graphic element that is part of the visual identity of a brand. .
